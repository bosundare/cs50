All About Me

Much about me was when I came into the United States. It was quite a jolly ride with quite a lot of ups and downs during my first few months in the United State. During my first few months, I was unable to get a decent paying job with my Chemistry degree, so I just had to resort to doing some other menial jobs jsut to make ends meet and fulfil my dream of earning some top dollars.

Some of the menial jobs I did was to work as a dog walker. Although, I was not a pet-friendly type of person, however, I was quite desperate to earn some money. Because of this, I had to walk dogs and also take care of cats.

During the day, I was a security guard working at H&M, while at night, I was an animal sitter, taking care of people's dog.

However, there was a time I was working as a security or what is usually known as Theft Prevention. One of the customer's tag was beeping. I was going to stop the customer, however, she thought I was grabbing her bag, so she slapped me right in the face.

This was when I decided it was time for me to move on to another career. I quit my job that same day and I decided to join the US Navy as an Electrician. This was the beginning of my career in the United States.
